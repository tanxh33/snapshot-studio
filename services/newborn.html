NEWBORN

Newborn ( 5-14 days old )

Newborns from 5 to 14 days old usually settle into cute poses and Snapshot will add an artistic touch with colours, florals and props. Family members and siblings can join in too!

Newborn ( 2 to 6 weeks old )

From 2 to 6 weeks old. newborns are still teeny tiny at that age and bright with sunny expressions as they move between dozily settling into poses and batting their eyes open to the world.

For babies 3-12 months old

Between 3-12 months, newborns are very playful and Snapshot loves to bring out their quirkiness through themes and costumes.
